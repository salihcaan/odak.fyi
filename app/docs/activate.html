Activate Odak

After you buy, your license is one click away. This page walks through exactly what you'll see in your inbox, where to find your key, and how to paste it into Odak.

3-step quickstart #

  1. 1 Open the email from Odak (via Polar). Subject line is "Your Odak order confirmation". It usually arrives within a minute of checkout.
  2. 2 Click the Access purchase button. It opens the Polar customer portal in your browser, signed in to your purchase.
  3. 3 Copy your license key, then open Odak → Settings → License and paste it in. Activation is instant.

The email from Polar #

Polar handles checkout and license delivery for Odak. Here's what the email looks like in your inbox:

O
Odak (via Polar) odak@notifications.polar.sh

Thank you for your purchase!

Thank you for purchasing Odak. Your invoice is attached. You can find your receipt and license in the Customer Portal.

Access purchase
↑ this is the button you want

The email itself doesn't contain your license key. Don't search the message body for a code — there isn't one. The key lives in the customer portal, one click behind the Access purchase button.

Inside the customer portal #

The portal shows your purchase, your invoices, and — under Benefits — a section called License. Reveal the key, copy it, and paste it into Odak:

  1. In Odak's menu bar icon → Settings…
  2. Select the License pane on the left.
  3. Paste your key into the field and hit Return.

The badge in Settings flips from Trial to Licensed immediately. No restart needed.

Activating a second or third Mac #

Your license activates on up to 3 Macs at the same time — typically a desktop, a laptop, and a work machine. To activate another:

  1. Sign in to the customer portal from any browser on the new Mac. The link is in your original purchase email; you can also reach it later at polar.sh with the same email address.
  2. Copy the same license key.
  3. Paste it into Settings → License on the new Mac.

There's no separate "activation count" you need to manage manually — Odak handles seat tracking in the background.

Moving the license to a new Mac #

Selling or retiring a Mac? You don't need to do anything special in advance. In Odak on the old machine, open Settings → License and click Deactivate this Mac. The seat frees up immediately and is ready to use on its replacement.

If you can't access the old Mac (lost laptop, hard drive failure), email support@odak.fyi and we'll release the seat from our side.

Trouble? #

I don't see the email

It usually arrives within a minute, but corporate spam filters sometimes hold it for longer. Check your spam/promotions folder first, then search your inbox for polar.sh. If it's still missing after 10 minutes, email support with the address you used at checkout and we'll resend it manually.

The email is there, but "Access purchase" doesn't work

The link signs you in to Polar using a one-time token in the URL. If it's expired or already used, request a fresh sign-in link from the Polar customer portal with the same email address you bought with.

Odak says "Invalid license"

Most often this is a whitespace issue — license keys sometimes pick up a trailing space when copied from email or browser. Try copying directly from the customer portal (it has a dedicated copy button) and re-pasting. If it still fails, email support and include the first 8 characters of your key.

I bought, but I'd like a refund

14 days, no questions. Reply to the order email or write to support@odak.fyi. See the full refund policy.

Still stuck? support@odak.fyi — usually a human reply within a few hours.
